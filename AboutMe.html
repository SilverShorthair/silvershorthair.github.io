I'm not sure if I willed this one into existence, but back in 2023 I bought Doom because of MyHouse.wad (very smart decision considering MyHouse needs Doom II) and predicted that I'd hyperfixate on Doom. I then proceeded to hyperfixate on Doom. Regardless of if I accidentally wasted some sort of genie wish on that or not, Doom is up there in my list of favorite things, and I absolutely love the entire franchise (with some exceptions).
The most recent thing to be added to the hyperfixation list is Inscryption. When I first started Inscryption back in July, I was extremely burnt-out and constantly stressed and exhausted, so I didn't get very far. It wasn't until November that I really started playing it, and holy shit it is a fantastic game. All I'll say is I wholeheartedly recommend it. Don't look up anything about it, don't watch anything about it (EVEN THE TRAILERS), just go buy it and play it. I promise you that this is an experience you'll want to go into completely blind.
