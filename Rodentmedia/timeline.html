Timeline of events

A relatively simplistic timeline of events for Rodentmedia:
  • [1976] Rodentmedia is founded, initially as a shovelware game developer.
  • [1988] Rodentmedia begins making software suites
  • [2001] RatWeb, Rodentmedia's official web browser, releases, alongside the official Rodentmedia forums.
  • [2001 - 2007] Yearly RatWeb releases.
  • [2008 - 2009] For 2 years, there are no new RatWeb releases, with Rodentmedia maintaining RatWeb 7 with security patches.
  • [2010] RatWeb 8 finally releases.
  • [2012] Rodentmedia goes out of business, and users wake up to find a 404 page where the forums once were.
